Our Mission

To bridge the gap between the general public and oral health workforce in the promotion of oral health and conservative dentistry, and prevention of NCDs in Africa.

Our Vision

A global organisation that spearheads oral and general health promotion and integration, and the advancement of the practice of dentistry in Africa.

Our Strategic Initiatives

Transforming oral health through purposeful action

01

Continuous Professional Development

Comprehensive programs for dental students and professionals in leadership and clinical dental practice. Empowering the next generation of African dental leaders.

02

African Dental Congress

A pan-African unity body bringing together African dental students and professionals for joint promotion of oral health and dentistry across the continent.

03

SMISSA Dental Society

School dental health clubs in St. Michael Secondary School, Soroti City, Eastern Uganda. The first of its kind in the country, fostering early awareness among young people.

04

Community Outreach Programs

Oral health awareness campaigns in schools and places of worship. Reached 2,000+ African dental students and 1,200+ practitioners across 16 African countries. In Uganda, covered 8 districts of Teso reaching 3,000 school children. Currently targeting 75,000+ people annually in Busia district, focusing on women of reproductive age and children.

Health Integration & Focus Areas

Why Health Integration Matters

Health is defined as a state of complete physical, mental, and social wellbeing‚Äînot merely the absence of disease. Oral health is an integral component of overall health that influences nutrition, communication, and quality of life.

Globally, 3.5 billion people suffer from oral diseases. Poor oral health is linked to serious conditions including diabetes, heart disease, and respiratory illness. Our focus is to strengthen oral health systems while promoting integrated NCD prevention.
